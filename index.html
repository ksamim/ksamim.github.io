Welcome to Otter Life!
Otter Life is an incremental, evolving game. Basics are revealed quickly, but new mechanics will slowly emerge. Your goal is to seek happiness, measured by Joy, earned by playing around and collecting food.
As you become more joyful, you will learn new things, allowing you to collect more resources, meet new friends, build more buildings, and much more. While you have spent most of your life on your safe riverbed, the journey for more Joy will lead to discoveries not just about yourself, but also about the wide world you live in...

Some Quick Tips!
Otter Life will be familiar to most players of idle, incremental, and evolving games. Nonetheless, here are some general tips to get you started!
  1. Click resources in the tab to buy new buildings
  2. Unlock new upgrades in the tab to become more powerful
  3. Change font, background, and other options in the micromenu
  4. A glowing tab means there's something to do there
  5. Check the micromenu for further questions!
